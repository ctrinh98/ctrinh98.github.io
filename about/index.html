About me

Nothing drives me more than people who work their hardest, try their best, and have the most fun while doing so.

I'm Christopher, a designer, developer, and all-around optimist. I currently study at Boston University in Boston, MA where I major in computer science. I'm especially passionate about clean design, virtual reality, and video games. I'm currently trying to help establish a game development community within my university with my best friends.

I love talking with people and learning what makes them tick. Get to know me and you'll find that I can't stop myself from learning new things, snacking on shrimp tempura and bubble tea, hanging out with my friends, or signing up for another cool hackathon. You can usually find me drawing and animating, reading about new tech stacks, or coming up with my next new idea.
